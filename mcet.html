Mohandas College of Engineering & Technology (MCET) is an engineering and technology college located at the village Anad, Thiruvananthapuram district, Kerala, India. The college, which is affiliated ti the KTU, was establihed in 2002. Mohandas College is a project of the Mohandas Group of Companies and is managed by the VN Gangandharan Panikker Memorial Trust. The college has received ISO 9001:2008 certification.
